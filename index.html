Who We Are

Our Purpose and Passion

We believe in designing spaces where people can feel truly connected to the moment, the place, and each other. A real home away from home. In a time when technology and remote lifestyles make us feel more isolated, we see an opportunity to create destinations that invite natural and authentic interaction and shared experiences. Our spaces are crafted to bring people together—whether it’s through a shared sunset view, a conversation around a communal table, watching sports events side by side or playing some pool to break the ice. We’re here to make meaningful connections possible in a world that needs them more than ever.

We’re passionate about reimagining hospitality for modern guests and their changing tastes and preferences. We blend the best of Thai culture for an authentic local experience with innovation and remarkable design to create unique spaces that resonate with customers. With decades of expertise and a deep understanding of Phuket’s vibrant tourism market, our team is dedicated to building iconic venues that leave a lasting legacy.

Our Mission

“Don't satisfy your customers, delight them ...”

Our mission is to push the boundaries of what hospitality can be. By blending sustainability, thoughtful design, and spaces that encourage genuine connection, we create properties that feel like a welcoming retreat that guests are drawn to again and again. Dream Machine is redefining what it means to travel in an increasingly open and changing world, setting new standards to shake off outdated preconceptions and elevate Phuket as a world-class, must-stay destination.

Our Approach

Precision and care in everything we do
